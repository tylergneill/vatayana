4	cit arthaḥ pratyayaḥ manyase artha naḥ vyāghātaḥ pratiṣedhaḥ pratipadyate pratyaya kva yuktam hetuḥ antaram viṣayaḥ sūtra uta abhidhīyate artham yuktaḥ nimitta sarvam vaktavyam vyāhatam kasmāt mātram upalabdhi viṣaya kadā doṣaḥ pratiṣidhyate asati anyaḥ bhavatā prāpnoti brūmaḥ aparijñānāt kasya śabda bhāvaḥ abhāvaḥ apare anya vartate bhavanti pratiṣedhāt anabhyupagamāt pratiṣedha

8	arthaḥ bhāṣya artha codanā kāreṇa vākya veda dharma pramāṇa artham bhāṣyam abhiprāyaḥ dharmaḥ vākyam arthāpattiḥ pratyakṣa śabda śabdaḥ darśayati sambandhaḥ bādhaḥ idānīm pūrva pramāṇam adhyayana sūtra antena śruta gṛha abhiprāyam vidyamāna avagamyate sambandha bahis kāraḥ lakṣaṇa vakṣyati vakṣyate prasiddha divā vyākhyā antara satyam upapadyate samprayoga anyathā dṛṣṭa arthāpatti rātri darśitaḥ
9	siddhiḥ sarva prasaṅgāt abhāvāt prasaṅgaḥ ayuktam pramāṇa siddhau virodhaḥ siddheḥ artha kasyacid asiddheḥ viśeṣa kiṃcid virodhāt tvayā bādhaka antara sarvatra dṛṣṭa sarvathā yuktam mātreṇa siddhi sādhanam anyathā sambhavati sarvam viruddha aviśeṣāt asiddha advaita aviśeṣe iṣṭa para viruddham vyavasthā abhāva loka abhyupagame anya kathaṃcid pakṣe āśraya ekānta sarvasya siddham asiddham anavasthā
10	śabda śabdaḥ artha śabdasya varṇa sambandhaḥ śabdāt arthaḥ vācya śabdānām vācaka śabdena sambandha artham śabde śabdam śabdāḥ varṇāḥ pada arthe varṇānām vākya pratītiḥ arthayoḥ vācakaḥ samaya krama pratīti saṅketa go padam śrotra vācyaḥ saṃketa saṃjñā vākyam cit vivakṣā abhidhāna sambandhe śabdayoḥ sphoṭa samayaḥ abhidheya kalpanā pratīyate pratipattiḥ dṛśyate prayujyate prayoga
11	sādhya pakṣa hetuḥ hetu dharma dṛṣṭāntaḥ sādhana vipakṣa hetoḥ kṛtaka asiddhaḥ dṛṣṭānta anvaya vyatirekaḥ udāharaṇa dharmaḥ anaikāntikaḥ śabdaḥ udāharaṇam vaidharmya pakṣe eka vṛttiḥ ubhaya viruddha sādharmya vipakṣāt vyatireka pakṣaḥ anyatara sādhye vipakṣe asiddha deśa anaikāntika dṛṣṭānte vyāpakaḥ vyāpti vipakṣaḥ anvayaḥ sandigdha viparīta vyāptiḥ vacanam anitya anityaḥ dharmiṇi amūrta gamaka vyāvṛttiḥ
12	abhāvaḥ abhāva bhāva anupalabdhiḥ svabhāva bhāvaḥ abhāve upalabdhi nivṛttiḥ anupalabdheḥ anupalambhaḥ lakṣaṇa abhāvam dṛśya anupalabdhi vastu kārya abhāvasya upalabdhiḥ abhāvāt prāṇa hetuḥ viruddha upalabhyate anya abhāvena siddhiḥ bhāve śīta asat upalambhaḥ svabhāvaḥ sattā upalambha sidhyati anupalambha pratiṣedha anupalambhāt sat pratiṣedhaḥ kāraṇa bhāvasya abhāvayoḥ anyathā anupalabdhyā vyāpaka pratibandhaḥ vyavahāra anupalabdhau r
13	dhūma agni anumānam dhūmaḥ liṅgam liṅga agniḥ sambandha deśa anumīyate agneḥ avinā sambandhaḥ anumāna gamaka kārya dhūmasya kāraṇa anumeya darśanāt vyāpti bhāvaḥ anyathā darśana grahaṇam vyāptiḥ dṛṣṭam viśeṣa anvaya viśiṣṭaḥ agnau dṛṣṭvā dhūmāt dṛṣṭaḥ darśanam pratipattiḥ abhāvaḥ agnim liṅgasya anumāne gamya anyatra sāmānyena agnimān dhūmam agninā pūrvavat sāmānyatas grahaṇa dharma
14	vidyā avidyā brahma ati alam su veda mahā śāstra sakala nāma vayam loka upāya sādhu sarva nyāya trayī panthāḥ dṛśyate ātmānam lokaḥ kṛtam patham āhuḥ uta dharma ācakṣate jana āgama vyākaraṇa ittham upāyaḥ kila vedāḥ vidaḥ matiḥ mayā śākhā loke adya kṛtaḥ atiśayaḥ me arhati bahu brahmaṇaḥ eti eṣā
15	artham lakṣaṇam lakṣaṇa arthaḥ artha prayojanam sūtram grahaṇam avadhāraṇam viśeṣa sāmānya tantra siddhāntaḥ labhyate sūtra abhidhānam jñāpana śabdena vyavaccheda pṛthak samāsaḥ nirdeśaḥ prayojana padam vākyam gamyate adhikaraṇa avadhāraṇa parīkṣā samāsa ṣaṣṭhī ukte viśeṣaṇa lakṣaṇasya vacanam vaktavyam uddeśaḥ padena vibhāga uddeśa prakaraṇam lakṣaṇe kartavyam abhidhānāt prakaraṇa sarva saṃjñā viśeṣaḥ śāstram śāstra
16	prāmāṇyam prāmāṇya veda pramāṇa āpta artha pramāṇam puruṣa svatas doṣa aprāmāṇyam vede mūla vaktṛ satya vākya apramāṇam paratas jñāna artham upadeśaḥ āgama apauruṣeya vedasya vākyam niścayaḥ para aprāmāṇya vedaḥ arthe prāmāṇye vākyānām niścaya apramāṇa sarvajña pauruṣeya antara vacanam kāraṇa anāpta praṇīta sarva upadeśa saṃvāda āgamaḥ vākyāni guṇa yathārtha śaṅkā āptaḥ
17	pratyakṣa pramāṇa anumānam pramāṇam anumāna pratyakṣam viṣaya prameya pratyakṣeṇa prameyam anumānasya liṅga artha anumānena phalam pratyakṣasya viṣayam pramāṇāni anumāne lakṣaṇa pūrvaka antaram lakṣaṇam para viṣayaḥ viṣaye pūrvakam artham pramāṇasya āgama jñānam anumānayoḥ upamāna sāmānya liṅgam antara parokṣa anumānāt pramīyate apratyakṣa apramāṇam prāmāṇyam phala pratyakṣaḥ antareṇa sambandha grahaṇam pratyakṣe pramāṇe pramāṇānām
18	guṇa dravya samavāyaḥ pṛthivī guṇaḥ dravyam rūpa āśraya guṇāḥ viśeṣa ākāśa sāmānya samavāya eka karma saṃkhyā āśrita saṃyoga guṇavat samavāyi nitya samavāyāt yuta samaveta kriyāvat ādhāra kāraṇa guṇānām ātma bhāvaḥ dravyeṣu abhāvāt sattā pṛthaktva saṃyukta sāmānyam vṛtti dravyasya tvañca paratva śabda dravye diś aneka buddhi rasa sat dravyāṇi parimāṇa ādiḥ
19	vidyate yujyate upapadyate phalam bhāvaḥ saṃbhavati utpādaḥ upādānam gantā nirvāṇam agniḥ anyaḥ vinā gamanam indhanam ātmā utpāda kṛtam pratipādayan pūrvam gacchati apekṣya para bhaviṣyati idānīm anyat saṃbhavaḥ darśana vibhavaḥ anya phalasya nāma siddhiḥ pratītya pṛthak staḥ ātmānam saha bhūtaḥ ṛte asat jāyate kiṃcid tiṣṭhati saṃsāraḥ bhavaḥ sadbhāvāt kārakaḥ upādāna duḥkham
20	jñāna kṛti viṣaya iṣṭa jñānam janya pravṛtti icchā janaka sādhana rajata sādhya jñāne kārya hetu vinā cikīrṣā abhāvāt dvārā anyathā kāraṇa jñānasya grahe siddha vācyam gauravāt jñānāt vyabhicārāt gocara pravartakam hetuḥ svatas māna vighna janyam viśiṣṭa mātra āpatteḥ grahaḥ viṣayaka lāghavāt sākṣāt asaṃsarga tādṛśa pravṛttiḥ sattvāt pāka sāmagrī dhvaṃsa bhrama
21	viśeṣaṇa viśeṣya viśiṣṭa viśeṣaṇam jñāna jñānam sadbhāve pratyaya rūpa sat nimitta sambandha asat daṇḍī abhāvāt bhāvaḥ nimittam abhāve janaka abhāvaḥ vyavahāraḥ utpattau vinā sattā upacāraḥ vyavahāra vyāvṛtta daṇḍa prasaṅgāt sambandhaḥ vācyam svarūpa dravya jñānasya alam bādhaka abhyupagame anyathā prasaṅgaḥ antara abhāva upapatteḥ upalambhāt sarvatra viṣaya viśeṣyam asambhavāt mukhya sambhavati samavāya
22	kasmāt kāraṇa kāryam kārya ayuktam antara kāraṇam anyat asat antaram bīja vikāra dadhi utpadyate matam sat prāk śakti prasaṃgaḥ kṣīra mṛd vikāraḥ utpatti nimitta aṅkura viṣāṇa prasaṃgāt anupapannam śaśa asataḥ vyaktam prakṛti aṅkuraḥ upādāna yuktam sataḥ kiṃca śāli tantu upalabhyate anupapatteḥ ghaṭa paṭaḥ kriyate brūmaḥ bījam prāc bhāvāt vyūha aniṣṭam
23	indriya manaḥ jñānam viṣaya manasaḥ sannikarṣa artha ātma pratyakṣam jñāna yugapad indriyam cakṣuḥ pratyakṣa karaṇa sukha vijñānam sannikarṣaḥ manasā utpadyate śrotra indriyāṇām indriyeṇa sannikarṣāt jam grahaṇam indriyasya mānasam bāhya vṛtti vṛttiḥ utpannam vyavasāya akṣam viṣayam kāraṇam akṣa smṛti vijñāna ja arthaḥ indriyāṇi antara grahaṇa sannikarṣasya prasaṅgaḥ jñānāni aparokṣa artham ayugapad
24	anvaya vākya pada artha padārtha śakti anvayaḥ anvita śaktiḥ padasya padam lakṣaṇā vinā sādhu saṃsarga abhāvāt padāt śakya pade bodhaḥ bodha padānām vibhakti bodhaka tātparya rūḍhi ityādau tīra anyathā prayoga eka ākāṅkṣā itara yogya lakṣaṇa vākyam saṅkhyā gaṅgā artham paṅkaja dhīḥ yogyatā tātparyam upasthitiḥ anvaye lakṣaṇayā vākye śābda arthasya prathamam

26	vastu vikalpa anya sāmānya sāmānyam viṣayaḥ rajatam apoha rajata śabda rūpam artha ākāra arthaḥ vikalpaḥ khyātiḥ pratibhāti viṣaya apohaḥ vyāvṛtti viparīta vastunaḥ rūpeṇa rūpa vyavaccheda abhāvāt avastu anyatra ākāraḥ lakṣaṇam vyavahāra vāsanā asat bāhya atad vikalpāḥ vikalpānām śuktikā vyāvṛttiḥ eka gṛhyate nāma pravṛttiḥ pratibhāsaḥ bhrānti pratibhāsa vyavahāraḥ buddhau dṛśya vastuni
27	rūpa cakṣuḥ sparśa rasa indriya gandha tvac grahaṇam rūpam indriyam upalabdhiḥ śabda sparśaḥ tejaḥ śrotra prāpya pradīpa ghrāṇa vāyuḥ indriyāṇi gṛhyate śrotram vāyu cakṣuṣā upalabhyate grāhya viṣaya aprāpya cakṣuṣaḥ kāri indriyāṇām pṛthivī dravya rūpasya indriyeṇa adhiṣṭhāna sparśasya vyañjaka rasana agrahaṇam āloka ādibhiḥ śīta grahaṇa karṇa udbhūta bhūta upalabdhi dravyam upalabdheḥ
28	nigraha sādhana sthānam pakṣa para artham pratijñā sthāna artha vacanam doṣa dūṣaṇa upālambhaḥ uttara vāde hetu chala pratipakṣa vādī kathā avayava sādhanam pratijña tarka tattva vacana pṛthak vādaḥ vādinaḥ uttaram aṅga udbhāvanam dūṣaṇam doṣaḥ siddhi jalpa tris apratipattiḥ vākyam abhidhānam asādhana nigamanam vādi nigṛhyate parājaya doṣam sthānāni punaruktam sthāpanā pratiṣedha
