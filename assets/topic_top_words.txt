1	pratītiḥ anyathā pratīyate rūpa asat nāma rūpeṇa para samvedanam samvedana gatiḥ pratipāditam abhāvāt aparam samāna antareṇa pūrva rūpam sakalam vastu pravarttate pareṇa anyena apara bhāvi pratipattiḥ svarūpa vyavahāraḥ samānam evantarhi paścāt apareṇa dṛśyate vedanam sambhavaḥ aparaḥ vyatirekeṇa bhrāntiḥ svarūpeṇa prāptam upalabhyate nna arthaḥ kathantarhi doṣaḥ pratīteḥ pratibhāsaḥ pramāṇam paraḥ pratibhāsa
2	arthaḥ abhiprāyaḥ darśayati bhāṣya abhiprāyeṇa idānīm sambhavati śakyate pūrvam sambandhaḥ antena avagamyate pūrva satyam bhaviṣyati kāraṇam āśaṃkya nāma kāreṇa kin ityādinā asmābhiḥ artha artham jñāna antaram asati bhāvaḥ siddham dvayena abhiprāyam vaktum darśitam sidhyati śabda prāk manyante anupapannam yāvat sambandha codayati pariharati mātram antareṇa darśitaḥ bhāṣyam prakaraṇāt dṛśyate anusāreṇa upapadyate
3	jñāna janya viṣaya jñānam kṛti janaka abhāvāt viśiṣṭa iṣṭa hetu anyathā jñāne vinā vācyam sādhana pramā viśeṣaṇa bhrama sādhya icchā jñānasya pravṛtti siddha kāraṇa rajata grahe hetuḥ gauravāt cikīrṣā āpatteḥ vṛtti grahaḥ vyabhicārāt ghaṭa bādhaka viṣayaka prakāraka sāmagrī māna dvārā pratibandhaka janyā sattvāt abhāva jñānāt janyam āvaśyaka prakāra anubhava aṃśe
4	svabhāva svabhāvaḥ bhāvaḥ bhāva hetu bhāvasya vināśaḥ prāk antara vināśa hetoḥ paścāt kṣaṇika svabhāvasya nāśaḥ hetuḥ kriyā kasyacid ayogāt nāśa sthiti kṣaṇa kadācid bhāvānām bhāve anyathā artha karoti kārya apara nāma prasaṅgāt svayam janana apekṣā prasaṅgaḥ svabhāvena bhāvāt vināśasya sahakāri vastu utpattiḥ apekṣa abhāvāt atad utpatteḥ svabhāvataḥ avaśyam kiṃcid utpāda
5	cit arthaḥ pratiṣedhaḥ manyase vyāghātaḥ hetuḥ artha naḥ yuktam kasmāt kāraṇam antaram pratiṣedha abhidhīyate pratiṣidhyate artham brūmaḥ ayuktam bhavatā vaktavyam doṣaḥ vyāhatam sūtra pratipadyate sarvam pratiṣedhāt abhāvaḥ anabhyupagamāt asati prāpnoti kadā prasaṅgaḥ kasya uta mātram sūtram yuktaḥ anupapattiḥ kva aparijñānāt traikālya viṣayaḥ etena ahetuḥ uttaram bruvāṇaḥ bhavataḥ nivartate prāptam vaktavyaḥ
6	ākāra nīla grāhya artha jñāna grāhaka ākāraḥ jñānam ākāram viṣaya rūpa arthaḥ jñānasya vedanam arthasya buddhiḥ anubhavaḥ bāhya ātma buddheḥ ākārasya rūpam vāsanā buddhi anubhava grāhyam vijñānam saṃvid nīlam ākārā bāhyaḥ dhīḥ saṃvedanam ākāreṇa vijñāna su sārūpyam tasyāḥ jñānena bahis anubhūyate saha saṃvittiḥ pīta dvi bodha grāhyatā citra jñāne sārūpya
7	saṃbandhaḥ śabdaḥ eka vidhīyate gamyate bhaviṣyati śrutiḥ vidhiḥ arthaḥ śabdena anuvādaḥ brūmaḥ vākyam vākya vacanam artham vākyena vidhānam guṇa vacanaḥ karma vidhātum arhati śrutyā śaknoti saṃbandham bhidyeta saṃbandha prakaraṇāt prakaraṇam artha śruti guṇaḥ pakṣe anarthakam śrūyate bhavitum saṃbandhasya vidhāna saṃbandhe avagamyate antaram prāyaścittam prayojanam ubhayam prāpte śakyate upāṃśu haviḥ stuti
8	dravya guṇa dravyam pṛthivī rūpa guṇaḥ viśeṣa guṇāḥ eka sāmānya saṃkhyā samavāyaḥ āśraya artham karma guṇavat samavāyi kāraṇa aneka ākāśa tvañca guṇānām kriyāvat āśrita dravyeṣu samaveta paratva pṛthaktva vṛtti bhidyate dravyasya lakṣaṇam bhāvaḥ sādharmyam samavāyāt pṛthaktvam itarasmāt parimāṇa dravyāṇi apekṣayā samavāya saṃyoga dravye samāna karmasu lakṣaṇa paramāṇu aparatva guṇeṣu grahaṇam
9	sādhya dharma pakṣa hetuḥ dharmaḥ hetu hetoḥ dharmi sādhana asiddhaḥ viruddha dharmī dharmiṇi pakṣaḥ pakṣe dharmasya asiddha lakṣaṇam sādhye anaikāntikaḥ viruddhaḥ anyatara vyāptiḥ sādhyaḥ anaikāntika dharmiṇaḥ eka avinā arthaḥ hetvābhāsaḥ lakṣaṇaḥ vyāpti lakṣaṇa mātra artham uktaḥ atyaya vipakṣāt tri vṛttiḥ vipakṣa siddhaḥ hetvābhāsāḥ dṛṣṭānta sādhyam siddha deśa dharmeṇa vipakṣe siddhau
10	sādhya pratijñā śāstra doṣaḥ sādhanam virodhaḥ sādhyam sādhana iṣṭa bādhā hetu pakṣa siddha asiddha dharmi sādhyaḥ dharmiṇaḥ dharma sādhyate siddhaḥ śāstram viruddhaḥ pakṣaḥ iṣṭaḥ svayam virodha bādhanam dharmiṇi abhyupagama viruddha sādhane siddham iṣṭam artha vādinā sādhanāt hetuḥ bādhane abhyupagamaḥ sādhayitum asiddham vacana nirddeśaḥ dharmī anyathā uktaḥ vādinaḥ iṣṭasya abhyupagamāt hetoḥ
11	kārya kāryam kāraṇa sāmagrī śakti bīja kāraṇam sahakāri mṛd śaktiḥ kāryasya aṅkura upādāna janana viśeṣa utpattiḥ eka antara cakṣuḥ hetu janayati śakteḥ sahakāriṇaḥ kāraṇāt śāli utpattau kāraṇasya vijñāna janaka svabhāva utpādana ghaṭa bījam viśeṣaḥ utpadyate utpāda pratyaya kārye kāri atiśaya jananam yogya kāraka kāryāt pūrva sahakāriṇām saha paraspara samanantara antaram
12	nāma alam ati dṛśyate mā arhati vayam ittham bhavitum vastu mātram sādhu śabda pakṣe kā bhaviṣyati nūnam anupapatteḥ uta saṃbhavāt ācakṣate kiṃcid kṛtam vaktum su matiḥ adya yuktam patham kṛtaḥ eṣā sūkṣma brūmaḥ asaṃbhavāt kila upāya kāmam padārtha panthāḥ asādhu satyam varam saṃbhavati sarvathā pratīti bhavān rūpam bhavatu kalpanā pratyabhijñā
13	vidhi pravṛtti vidhiḥ artha phala vidheḥ svarga niyogaḥ sādhana niyoga arthaḥ puruṣa adhikāra antara pramāṇa pravṛttiḥ liṅā viṣaya nā bhāvanā upāya kāma śabda cā vākya aṅga sādhya yāga kartavya pravartaka anyathā phalam pravartate pravṛtteḥ apekṣa svarūpa kārya kriyā kāmya loke niyojya vyāpāraḥ bhūta avagamāt puruṣaḥ prayojana viniyoga vidhau viṣayaḥ niṣedha
14	eka aneka ekam rūpa ekasya bhedaḥ bhinna nānā bheda rūpam vastu svabhāva ekaḥ paraspara dharma ātma abhinna ekatra anekam rūpeṇa vastunaḥ ekasmin bhedāt ekena bhede viruddha ātmanā bhinnaḥ anya antara dvayoḥ dvi dravya prasaṅgaḥ ekasmāt virodhaḥ svabhāvaḥ parasparam prasaṅgāt abheda bhinnam dvaya rūpasya bhedena abhedaḥ paryāya ātmanaḥ rūpaḥ abhinnam ātmakam
15	vastu artha vikalpa sāmānya sāmānyam viṣayaḥ anya lakṣaṇam kriyā rūpa śabda rūpam vastuni viṣaya apoha vyāvṛtti lakṣaṇa arthaḥ vikalpaḥ atad vikalpasya vastunaḥ avastu apohaḥ buddhi ākāra maṇi vyāvṛttiḥ vikalpānām jñāna rūpeṇa vijātīya vikalpena kalpita ākāraḥ anartha buddhiḥ viṣayā rūpasya vidhi vyāvṛtta saṃketa tasyāḥ arthe keśa lakṣaṇe sāmānyasya viṣayam vyāvṛttam dhiyaḥ
16	anumānam liṅga dhūma agni liṅgam deśa sambandha dhūmaḥ anumāna agniḥ anumīyate sambandhaḥ anumeya dṛṣṭam vyāpti liṅgasya avinā agneḥ liṅgi dhūmasya sāmānyatas darśanāt eka anumānena dhūmāt anumāne pratyakṣa liṅgāt viśeṣa viśiṣṭaḥ dṛṣṭa dṛṣṭvā anumānāt dhūmam pūrvavat sāmānyena viśeṣeṇa agninā pratipattiḥ viśiṣṭa sāmānyatodṛṣṭam dharma grahaṇam anumānasya kāla pūrvaka darśana deśaḥ parvata pratyakṣeṇa
17	pramāṇa pratyakṣa anumāna pramāṇam anumānam pratyakṣam anumānasya pratyakṣeṇa viṣaya prameya anumānena pratyakṣasya prāmāṇyam antara siddhiḥ āgama viṣayaḥ pramāṇena apramāṇam artha viṣaye vastu viṣayam pramāṇasya anumānāt anumāne abhāvāt bādhakam parokṣa siddheḥ bādhaka lakṣaṇa bādhyate apramāṇa antaram anumānayoḥ sāmānya bala antareṇa loka viśeṣa pratyakṣāt grāhi siddham pramāṇāt pūrvaka pramāṇāni siddhau bādhita anavasthā
18	īśvara kartṛ īśvaraḥ ghaṭa kārya śarīra sarva siddhiḥ kṣiti jagat asmad īśvarasya sarvajña viśeṣa buddhimat ayuktam adṛṣṭa pūrvaka prasaṅgāt buddhi sanniveśa prasaṅgaḥ āśraya icchā kāryam yuktam abhāvāt viśeṣaḥ kiṃcid kāraṇa upādāna mātreṇa kulāla upalambhāt siddhau nimitta sarga aviśeṣe nitya kasyacid darśanāt siddheḥ kartā saṃsthānam acetana vaicitryam sthāvara puruṣa saṃsthāna sādhanam
19	kasmāt puruṣa sattva pradhāna prakṛti puruṣasya pradhānam puruṣaḥ rajaḥ buddhi antara antaram ayuktam tamaḥ prasaṃgaḥ prakṛtiḥ guṇa buddhiḥ pañca prasaṃgāt pradhānasya sargaḥ pariṇāmaḥ bhūta mahat vikāra dharma matam avyaktam kārya anyat vyaktam kiṃca vṛtti buddheḥ guṇānām tattva pravartate rūpa pravṛttiḥ vṛttiḥ viṣaya prakṛteḥ bhāva śabda kāryam śakti avasthā bhoktṛ sattvam
20	śabda artha śabdaḥ śabdasya śabdāt vācya arthaḥ vācaka sambandha artham sambandhaḥ śabdānām arthayoḥ arthe śabdena anyathā pratītiḥ saṅketa samaya saṃketa śabdāḥ śabde vivakṣā śabdam go pratipattiḥ vācakaḥ arthasya icchā arthāpattiḥ pratīti sambandhasya abhidhāna abhidheya samayaḥ saṃketaḥ antara pūrvikā abhāvāt vyavahāra artheṣu anupapattyā saṅketaḥ vācyaḥ niyamaḥ antareṇa kalpanā prayoga arthena puruṣa
21	rūpa jñāna prakāśa ākāra ātma jñānam ayuktam vijñāna svarūpam para prakāśaḥ vijñānam saṃvedanam jñānasya ātmā bodha svarūpa artha ātmanaḥ ātmaka rūpam upapadyate antara prakāśate antaram prasaṅgaḥ saṃvedana ātmānam jñānena viṣaya ātmani āhosvit arthaḥ kriyate rūpeṇa prakāśaka eka sattā pradīpa svayam santāna ātmakam vijñānasya ātmanā utpādyate saha saṃvedya ajñāta ghaṭa arthasya
22	duḥkha tattva karma sukha duḥkham sukham avidyā mokṣa brahma mokṣaḥ ātma janma apavargaḥ saṃsāra śarīra pravṛtti jñāna bhoga bandha apavarga nivṛttiḥ karmaṇām brahmaṇaḥ sarva jñānāt phalam doṣa ātmā phala kleśa ānanda jñānam tapaḥ mukti nitya vidyā pravṛttiḥ artham mithyājñāna rāga niḥśreyasa ātmanaḥ yoga adharma saṃsāraḥ kṣayaḥ mithyā sukhasya kṛta abhāvāt
23	kārya kāraṇa bhāvaḥ kāryam kāraṇam bhāva abhāve svabhāva bhāve dhūma bhāvāt dhūmaḥ kāryasya pratibandhaḥ kāraṇe abhāvāt gamaka vṛkṣa anvaya hetuḥ agni śiṃśapā svabhāvaḥ hetu janya kāraṇasya vyatireka niyamaḥ tādātmya anyathā hetau vinā kāraṇāt gamya dhūmasya niyama bhāvasya kāraṇayoḥ kāraṇena sāmagrī indhana akārya janyaḥ avaśyam avyabhicāraḥ tādṛśaḥ tādātmyam abhāvaḥ antareṇa janaka
24	jñānam pramāṇam pramāṇa lakṣaṇam lakṣaṇa phalam pratyakṣa pratyakṣam prameyam viṣaya artham artha phala viṣayam prameya jñāna sat jñānasya arthaḥ pramāṇāni indriya anumāna samprayoga viśeṣaṇa anyat utpadyate sannikarṣa lakṣaṇasya sāmānya lakṣaṇe jam iṣṭam pramīyate animitta pramāṇasya svarūpam liṅga samprayogaḥ pramāṇe avyapadeśyam vidyamāna sādhanam vijñānam ātmakam pramitiḥ pratyakṣasya grahaṇam pūrvakam viśeṣaṇam lakṣya
25	sarva nirvāṇam śūnyatā ārya bhāvānām bhagavatā svabhāvaḥ vidyate bhāvāḥ lokaḥ svabhāva bhāva śūnyam dharmāḥ sarvam pratītya śūnyāḥ grāhaḥ śūnya skandha māyā dharma dharmāṇām niḥsvabhāva śūnyatām bhaviṣyati santi nirodhaḥ tathāgataḥ svabhāvena aśūnyam ubhayam paśyati yujyate dharmān tathāgata saṃbhavati kasya upādāya svabhāvam śāśvata dṛṣṭayaḥ bhagavān viparyāsa sūtre kiṃcid svabhāvataḥ śūnyaḥ upapadyate mahā
26	indriya pratyakṣam pratyakṣa jñānam kalpanā akṣa artha viṣaya manaḥ jam mānasam vikalpakam jñāna sannikarṣa vikalpaka vijñānam aparokṣa viṣayam akṣam nirvikalpaka indriyasya pratyakṣasya indriyam cakṣuḥ vikalpa ja indriyeṇa apoḍham arthaḥ nirvikalpakam mānasa viṣaye viṣayaḥ apratyakṣa śabda vijñāna vyāpāra jñānasya jā artham arthe gocaraḥ grahaṇam vikalpaḥ spaṣṭa vyavasāya utpadyate anindriya rūpa sannikarṣāt
27	arthaḥ anyaḥ viśeṣaḥ puruṣaḥ viṣayaḥ dharmaḥ yam uktaḥ svayam paśyati ādiḥ dṛṣṭaḥ ātmā artham nāma enam ekaḥ anyena rūpaḥ vyatiriktaḥ doṣaḥ san pratyayaḥ idānīm aham pratipadyate arthasya siddhaḥ bhūtaḥ nava aparaḥ lakṣaṇaḥ gataḥ mayā avagamyate sarvaḥ yasmin anyasya tasmin samarthaḥ āśrayaḥ kena jānāti dṛśyate kasya itaraḥ svabhāvaḥ kṛtaḥ paraḥ mātram
28	phalam svarga phala yajeta kāmaḥ karma darśa yāgaḥ pradhāna guṇa yāga aṅga svargaḥ arthaḥ aṅgam paśu agnihotram juhoti apūrvam kratu puruṣa śrūyate kāmasya yāgena juhuyāt karmaṇaḥ yāgasya pradhānam dravya yāgam pūrṇamāsābhyām dravyam gamyate pūrṇamāsa śeṣa śākhā artham agnihotra kartavyatā apūrva prati kuryāt kāla phalavat yajati kāma kartavya bhāvaḥ itikartavya svargam
29	anupalabdhiḥ abhāva upalabdhi upalabdhiḥ anupalabdhi anupalabdheḥ lakṣaṇa abhāvaḥ upalambha anupalambhaḥ svabhāva dṛśya upalambhaḥ upalabhyate anupalambha viruddha vyavahāra asat śīta siddhiḥ anya anupalabdhyā anupalambhāt pratiṣedha anupalabdhau bhāva vyāpaka prāptasya yogya upalambhāt sādhyate abhāvāt pratiṣedhaḥ hetuḥ viṣaya abhāvam yā vyavahāraḥ upalabdheḥ āvaraṇa prakāra prāpta kārya adṛśya prasajya hetu vivikta sidhyati prayogaḥ abhāvasya
30	cit kva iṣyate vastu pratīyate matiḥ dhīḥ śabda vinā naḥ buddhiḥ kiñ yā prasajyate vācya kalpanā gamyate arthe vidyate eṣā prati asādhāraṇa kena yujyate pūrva kalpyate yogya prāk mātra bhinna buddhi cana ṛte iṣṭam śakti bhedaḥ kathan jāyate sarva candra kaiḥ ke vṛttiḥ dhruvam mṛṣā dṛśyate vāryate anya kathyate apohya
31	sāmānyam vyakti sāmānya jāti go sāmānyasya viśeṣa vyaktiḥ jātiḥ vyakteḥ pratyaya jāteḥ viśeṣaḥ ākṛti vyaktau anugata vyaktīnām anuvṛtti gauḥ vyāvṛtti saṃsthāna sāmānye vyaktayaḥ samāna eka vyāvṛtta sarva vyaktiṣu piṇḍa ākṛtiḥ śabda buddhiḥ vyāvṛttiḥ sāmānyena pratyayaḥ vyañjaka jātau śābaleya aśva sarvatra viśeṣeṣu viśeṣāṇām antare anugama gavā jātim vyaktim śāvaleya sāmānyāt vartate
32	jñānam artha rajata jñānasya jñāna candra rajatam ālambana ālambanam bhrānta udaka pratibhāti khyātiḥ viparīta jñāne bhrāntam viṣaya śuktikā bhrānti kalpanā bhrāntiḥ bādhaka asat prasaṅgāt deśa viṣayam ākāra anyathā nirviṣaya dvi udakam avisaṃvādi jala abhrānta atīta anya avyabhicāri ayuktam ākāraḥ prāpaka nirviṣayam samyak abhra avidyamāna grāhi anurodhena viparyayaḥ ākāreṇa abhāvāt antara
33	anvaya vyatireka vyatirekaḥ prāṇa nivṛttiḥ anvayaḥ abhāve abhāvaḥ abhāva hetoḥ nivṛtti vipakṣe hetuḥ mātreṇa bhāva jīvat vyāvṛttiḥ niścayaḥ kevala vipakṣāt ātma vyatirekī vyatirekayoḥ sidhyati nivṛttau vyatirekau adarśana vipakṣa siddhiḥ vyatirekasya asiddheḥ hetu vyatirekābhyām sandigdha gatiḥ pakṣāt gamakaḥ sattvam vyāvṛtteḥ ādiḥ gamaka niścita śrāvaṇa vyāvṛtti asati ātmā vyatirekiṇaḥ anvayī abhāvāt ghaṭa
34	sādhya vyāpti vyāpya vyāpaka abhāva pakṣa vṛtti vahni upādhiḥ vyāptiḥ dhūma dharma vyabhicāra adhikaraṇa avyāpaka sādhana anumiti upādhi pratiyogi pakṣe vācyam vyartha vyāpakam pṛthivī sat anumitiḥ abhāvāt vyatireki janya vinā viśeṣaṇa bādha vyāpteḥ atyanta avyāpya bahis anumāna dūṣaka sādhaka anyonya daśāyām vipakṣa mātra upādheḥ abhāvaḥ avacchinna hetu vyāpyam anyathā pratipakṣa
