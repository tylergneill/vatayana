a window onto the Sanskrit pramāṇa-NLP corpus

(note: a "doc" , i.e., document is roughly a paragraph)





Go Deeper


Vātāyana illuminates intertextual connections by performing detailed pairwise comparison (TF-IDF, Smith-Waterman) on a targeted subset of all corpus documents (currently ≈ 22k). This preliminary targeting is accomplished both with LDA topic comparison, which identifies the most "topically relevant" %15 of documents, and (more simply) with user-specific text prioritization. Depending on the specific use case, however, the first of these constraints (topic similarity) may not be desired. The controls below are meant to encourage advanced users to experiment and find what works best for them.
