Go Deeper


Vātāyana illuminates intertextual connections by performing pairwise comparisons of corpus documents (current total ≈ 22k docs) of three different sorts. Comparison by LDA topics is fast and captures abstract semantics, whereas TF-IDF and Smith-Waterman home in on word-level correspondences, albeit at relatively greater computational cost. The advanced settings below aim to accomodate various use cases by allowing users to control:

  1. the number of expensive TF-IDF and Smith-Waterman comparisons to perform, both in normal ("shallow") and "deep" search modes;
  2. the subset of texts to restrict these more expensive comparisons to; and
  3. the qualitative interpretation of individual LDA topics, as well as their relative weighting during preliminary document comparison by topics.
As all of these factors gradually become clearer by using the site, users are encouraged to experiment to find which parameters work best for them.
