More info

The system compares documents using fast topic modeling, slower TF-IDF, and slowest Smith-Waterman methods. Limits control how many comparisons occur at each step, balancing speed and quality.

Top similarity scores from TF-IDF and Smith-Waterman are saved to speed up future searches. By default, up to 500 TF-IDF and 25 Smith-Waterman results are stored, increasing over time to limits of 4000 and 200. Choosing values within these limits ensures faster results later; higher values are processed but not saved.

Time estimates apply to one docExplore query. docCompare takes roughly twice as long.
